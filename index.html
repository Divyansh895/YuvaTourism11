What is Yuva Tourism Club(YTC)?



Yuva Tourism Club (YTC) is an initiative of the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, designed to create interest, awareness, and a sense of responsibility towards the country's natural and cultural heritage/tourism among children and youth. The vision is to nurture and develop young ambassadors of Indian tourism who would become aware of tourism possibilities in India, appreciate the rich cultural heritage, and develop an interest and passion for tourism.

YTC is open to all students aged 10-25 years. The clubs are registered with the Ministry of Tourism and are affiliated with the State Tourism Departments. Each club is managed by a Coordinator, typically a teacher or staff member of the school/college.

The YTC clubs organize various activities to promote tourism awareness and appreciation among the youth. These activities include:


  • Field trips to tourist destinations

  • Heritage walks

  • Cultural performances

  • Photo exhibitions

  • Film screenings

  • Quiz contests and debates

  • Essay writing and painting competitions

  • Awareness campaigns on responsible tourism

The YTC clubs also play a crucial role in promoting sustainable tourism practices. Members are encouraged to adopt eco-friendly travel habits and to be respectful of the local environment and culture.

Joining a Yuva Tourism Club offers numerous benefits:


  • Learn about the tourism industry and its potential as a career option

  • Develop skills and talents in areas such as tourism marketing, event management, and customer service

  • Gain exposure to different cultures and traditions

  • Make new friends and network with other young people interested in tourism

  • Contribute to the promotion of tourism in India

If you are a student between the ages of 10 and 25, we encourage you to join a Yuva Tourism Club. It is an excellent way to learn, grow, and make a difference.
